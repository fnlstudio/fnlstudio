How to Buy Our Presets

Follow these simple steps to purchase a preset from FNL Studio:

Step 1: Take a Screenshot
Go to the "Our Presets" section and take a screenshot of the preset you want to buy.

Step 2: Message Us on Instagram
Send us a message on Instagram at @fnl_studio_ with the following:

  • Preset Name
  • Price
  • Screenshot of the preset

Step 3: Make Payment
We’ll provide payment details via Instagram DM. Follow the instructions to complete the payment.

Step 4: Receive the Preset
Once payment is confirmed, we’ll send you the .DNG preset file for download.
